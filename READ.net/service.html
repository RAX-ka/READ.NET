Frequently Asked Questions

How does the personalized recommendation service work?

Our AI analyzes your reading history, ratings, and preferences to identify patterns. We combine this with human curation from our literary experts to deliver recommendations that match your unique tastes. The more you use our platform, the better our suggestions become.

Can I access audiobooks on multiple devices?

Yes! Our audiobook service allows you to listen on up to 5 devices simultaneously. Your progress syncs across all devices, so you can switch from your phone to your tablet to your computer seamlessly.

How does the book exchange program work?

Simply list books you're willing to trade on our platform. When another member requests one of your books, you'll receive shipping instructions. For each book you send, you earn credits to request books from other members. We handle the logistics to make swapping books easy and efficient.

Are there any additional costs for joining book clubs?

All book clubs are free for READ.net members. Some clubs may choose books that require purchase, but we always offer library and used book options. Premium subscribers get 15% off all book club selections.
