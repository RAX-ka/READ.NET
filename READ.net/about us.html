Who We Are

From a Small Bookstore to a Global Community

Founded in 2015, READ.net began as a small independent bookstore with a simple mission: to help people discover books they'll love. What started as a single storefront has grown into a vibrant online community of over 500,000 readers worldwide.

Our journey began when our founder, Sarah Johnson, noticed how difficult it was for readers to find books that truly resonated with them. She envisioned a place where book lovers could not only find their next great read but also connect with fellow readers who shared their passions.

Today, we're proud to have built a platform that combines cutting-edge technology with human curation to deliver personalized book recommendations, foster meaningful literary discussions, and support authors at every stage of their careers.
